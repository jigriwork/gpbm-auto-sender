# Public Homepage

The public homepage positions GPBM Auto Sender around this core promise:

> Send every bill on WhatsApp automatically, without changing your billing software.

## Sections

- Premium header with nav anchors and login CTA.
- Hero with product promise, supporting explanation, and dashboard mockup.
- Problem section covering integration cost, manual sending, customer expectations, and owner tracking needs.
- Solution section covering folder watcher, PDF extraction, provider layer, duplicate guard, retry queue, and dashboard.
- How it works section with five steps from saved PDF to tracked WhatsApp bill.
- Compatibility section for Generic PDF Folder Watcher, Logic, Marg, Tally, Custom PDF, and provider adapters.
- Multi-store SaaS section explaining business/store/provider/template/parser/agent scoping.
- Pricing placeholder with Starter, Growth, Scale, and Enterprise.
- FAQ and footer.

## Known Gaps

The homepage uses UI mockups rather than real screenshots. Pricing is intentionally placeholder until the business model is decided.
